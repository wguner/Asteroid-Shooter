Asteroid Shooter

Team Members: Lucas Mason, 
			  Sean Roberts, 
			  Setenay Guner

Instructions:
	Press A to move left.
	Press D to move right.
	Press Spacebar to shoot.
	Try not to let any Asteroids reach the bottom of the screen.
	You have three lives! Survive as long as possible!
